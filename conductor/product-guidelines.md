# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **Tone:** Professional, helpful, and community-oriented.
- **Language:** Clear and concise, avoiding jargon.
- **Audience:** Both local Azerbaijani users and visitors.

## Branding
- **Name:** Yolmates (Azerbaijani for "on the way").
- **Visual Identity:** Clean, modern, and reliable.
- **Consistency:** Ensure uniform terminology across all platforms (e.g., "Sürücü" for Driver, "Sərnişin" for Passenger).

## UX Principles
- **Clarity First:** Information about rides, drivers, and costs must be immediately clear.
- **Seamless Flow:** Minimize the number of steps required to find and book a ride.
- **Trust and Safety:** Clearly indicate verified profiles and reviews to build confidence.
- **Accessibility:** Ensure the interface is usable for all target audiences, including those with varying levels of tech proficiency.

## Design Patterns
- **Mobile-First:** Prioritize the experience on mobile devices, as carpooling is often managed on the go.
- **Responsive Design:** Maintain a consistent and high-quality experience across all device sizes.
